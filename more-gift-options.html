Poster – Symbols of the Human Body

Poster of human body symbols from the chapter Temple of Ahenún in the book Ar Meru. The symbols were created with ink and brush, then digitized. The colored background is a scan of a hand-crafted imitation of old paper. Layout and titles were arranged in Word 2003.

T-Shirt – Hetem Mefter (Key to the Heavens)

Shirt featuring the Hetem Mefter diagram. The inner ring shows the Sun's movement relative to the Earth, reflecting day-night phases and seasons. The middle four rings consist of 256 symbolic figures derived from four variations of one symbol, digitally multiplied and rotated. They represent 64 energetic transformations across the seasons — the Celestial Symbols. The outer ring shows 64 bodily symbols – the Earth Symbols – selected from the inner rings. Inked by hand, digitized, and completed in Inkscape after a year-long process.

Map – World of Metérnes

This fictional map was hand-drawn and scanned. It portrays the imaginary world of Metérnes, birthplace of the ancestors of the characters in Ar Meru. A quiet artifact from the inner geography of myth and invention.
